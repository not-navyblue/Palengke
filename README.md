# Palengke
An UnCiv mod that adds a unique building to existing modded Philippine civilizations.

### Requirements
This mod is incompatible with the "Civ V - Vanilla" ruleset.<br>
Additionally, this mod does absolutely nothing when used on its own. It is required that you must have enabled at least one 
compatible mod for it to be usable in normal gameplay, then play as one of the Civilizations as listed below:
- The Philippines
	- Relevant Mod: [*"ph bonifacio unciv" by shannaurelle*](https://github.com/shannaurelle/ph-bonifacio-unciv)
		- Civilization: Andres Bonifacio of Philippines
- Filipinas
	- Relevant Mod: [*"Project Felipe" by Angelonius*](https://github.com/Angelonius/Project-Felipe)
		- Civilization: Felipe I of Filipinas
- Tondo
	- Relevant Mods (PICK ONLY ONE, OR YOU WILL HAVE WEIRD RESULTS.)
		- [*"Project Tondo" by Angelonius*](https://github.com/Angelonius/Project-Tondo)
			- Civilization: Bunao Lakandula of Tondo
		- [*"Nusantara Civilizations" by amfauzn*](https://github.com/amfauzn/Nusantara-Civilizations)
			- Civilization: Kalangitan of Tondo
			- **NOTE: When planning to play as Tondo with Nusantara Civilizations, select that mod first before selecting the Palengke mod.**
- Cebu
	- Relevant Mod: [*"Nusantara Civilizations" by amfauzn*](https://github.com/amfauzn/Nusantara-Civilizations)
		- Civilization: Humabon of Cebu 
- Sulu
	- Relevant Mod: [*"Nusantara Civilizations" by amfauzn*](https://github.com/amfauzn/Nusantara-Civilizations)
		- Civilization: Azim Ud-Din I of Sulu 
- Maguindanao
	- Relevant Mod: [*"Nusantara Civilizations" by amfauzn*](https://github.com/amfauzn/Nusantara-Civilizations)
		- Civilization: Kudarat of Maguindanao
- Igorot
	- Relevant Mod: [*"Nusantara Civilizations" by amfauzn*](https://github.com/amfauzn/Nusantara-Civilizations)
		- Civilization: Aliguyon of Igorot
- Madja-as
	- Relevant Mod: [*"Nusantara Civilizations" by amfauzn*](https://github.com/amfauzn/Nusantara-Civilizations)
		- Civilization: Sumakwel of Madja-as


## Mod Contents
### Buildings
#### Palengke
- Replaces Market
- Unique to\*\*: Philippines, Filipinas, Tondo, Cebu, Sulu, and Maguindanao
- Gold: 1 vs 2
- Cost: 125 vs 100
- 20% Gold vs 25% Gold
- Unique Effects:
	- +1 Food, +1 Gold, and +1 Production from each Trade Route
	- +1 Production and +1 Gold from tiles with Stone
	- +1 Food and +1 Gold from other types of bonus resource tiles
	- +1 Production and +1 Food from tiles with Horses
	- +1 Production and +1 Gold from other types of strategic resource tiles
	- +1 Gold, +1 Production from luxury resource tiles

<span style="font-size:11px;color:gray">\*\* Each Civ has an individual replacement that is exactly identical to each other.</span>
